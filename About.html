"Until we get equality in education , we won't have an equal society."


The Vidya Foundation, an initiative for the children to provide quality education and help them . The main aim of the Vidya Foundation is to remove deprivation & bring out youth by making them independent so that they can live a happy & normal life through the most powerful tool i.e. education..We are a non profitable organisation with the vision to provide "Right to Education" and quality education for all the children's of the society. Generally, we aim at bringing happiness to the society through spreading the idea of awareness, as happy society is always healthy society. We tend to influence & encourage them, thus removing the difference & mentality between rich & poor. Our mission is to spread our idea of awareness through mutual efforts at online & ground level, and help as many needy we can. possible has inspired many people and they have joined hands to create a better society for living. Join us in our journey and lets make the world a better place to live for all.
